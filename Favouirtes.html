BONOO INDIAN TAPAS

Bonoo Restaurant is on a mission to change the way they serve by sharing tapas style Indian food. The restaurant was formed in 2016. It is named after the owner's son's baby nickname 'Bonoo'. Friendly and cozy as their ovens in the kitchen, Nora Cafe is a Traditional English Cafe. With scrumptious breakfasts; to deep rich Coffees, and mouth delighting desserts; the heart of London has never been tastier.
